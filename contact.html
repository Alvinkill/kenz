Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear?

Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing that's easy to remove. You'll undress to your comfort level, and we provide premium linens for full coverage and privacy.

How long should I stay afterward?

Most clients benefit from 10–15 minutes of rest post-session. We have a comfortable lounge area with water and herbal tea available.

How often should I get a massage?

It depends on your goals. For maintenance and relaxation, once monthly is ideal. For injury recovery or athletes, weekly sessions during active training is recommended.
