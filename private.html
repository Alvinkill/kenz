18+ Private Massage

We offer exclusive, one‑on‑one massage sessions designed specifically for adults. These experiences are intended for women seeking a quiet, intimate atmosphere with professionally trained therapists. All clients must be at least 18 years of age; ID verification is required at booking.

What You Can Expect

  • Discreet, private treatment rooms with soft lighting and calming music.
  • Therapists skilled in creating a comfortable, respectful environment.
  • Personalized pressure and technique to promote deep relaxation and connection.
  • A focus on relaxation, stress relief, and emotional wellbeing.
  • Strict confidentiality and boundaries maintained at all times.

Age & Gender Guidelines

These sessions are reserved for adults (18+) and are primarily tailored toward female clients. We require a valid ID upon arrival and encourage open communication about comfort levels before the session begins.

Booking & Contact

To schedule a private session or ask questions, please contact us or call our studio. Walk-ins are not available for these services; appointments must be made in advance.

Kenz Massage & Wellness upholds a professional code of conduct. Any behaviour that compromises the safety or dignity of our staff or clients will result in termination of the session without refund.
